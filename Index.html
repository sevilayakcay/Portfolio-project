Welcome to my page

Hi, I’m Sevilay Guns, a frontend developer with a strong foundation in design and a background in the arts. My journey into web development started with a passion for creating visually engaging experiences, and I bring that same creativity and eye for detail into every project I build.

I specialize in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (with growing experience in modern frameworks), crafting responsive, user-friendly interfaces that balance functionality with aesthetics. My artistic background helps me approach code with a design-first mindset—whether it’s choosing harmonious color palettes, shaping intuitive layouts, or ensuring accessibility.

I’m especially interested in projects where technology meets creativity: interactive websites, digital art showcases, and applications that not only work well but also look and feel inspiring.
